Scoring rules:

- Use the full -3 to +3 scale.
- +3: voice-like, concise, and plain text.
- +2: wordy but still spoken and plain text (no formatting).
- 0: invalid/not applicable.
- -1: stiff or overly formal.
- -2: robotic.
- -3: uses formatting that makes it unreadable aloud.
