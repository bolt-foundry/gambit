# AcmeFlow Official FAQ (Knowledge Base)

Use this knowledge base to answer retrieval requests. Each entry includes a
stable `id`, exact question/answer text, and official source URL.

## Company Overview

- `company_overview.what_is_acmeflow`
  - **Question**: What is AcmeFlow?
  - **Answer**: AcmeFlow is a workflow automation platform that helps mid-sized
    teams design, automate, and monitor internal business processes without
    writing code.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/company-overview#what-is-acmeflow
- `company_overview.who_is_acmeflow_for`
  - **Question**: Who is AcmeFlow for?
  - **Answer**: AcmeFlow is designed for operations, finance, and IT teams at
    companies with 50-1,000 employees.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/company-overview#who-is-it-for

## Plans & Pricing

- `plans_pricing.what_plans_are_available`
  - **Question**: What pricing plans does AcmeFlow offer?
  - **Answer**: AcmeFlow offers three plans: Starter, Professional, and
    Enterprise.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/plans-and-pricing#plan-overview
- `plans_pricing.how_much_does_acmeflow_cost`
  - **Question**: How much does AcmeFlow cost?
  - **Answer**: Pricing starts at $49 per user per month on the Starter plan.
    Professional and Enterprise pricing varies based on usage and features.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/plans-and-pricing#pricing
- `plans_pricing.annual_billing`
  - **Question**: Do you offer annual billing?
  - **Answer**: Yes. Annual billing is available and includes a 10% discount
    compared to monthly billing.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/plans-and-pricing#annual-billing
- `plans_pricing.free_trial`
  - **Question**: Is there a free trial?
  - **Answer**: Yes. AcmeFlow offers a 14-day free trial with access to the
    Starter plan features.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/plans-and-pricing#free-trial

## Billing & Payments

- `billing_payments.payment_methods`
  - **Question**: What payment methods do you accept?
  - **Answer**: AcmeFlow accepts major credit cards. ACH payments are available
    on the Enterprise plan.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/billing-and-payments#methods
- `billing_payments.billing_schedule`
  - **Question**: When am I billed?
  - **Answer**: Customers on monthly plans are billed at the start of each
    billing cycle. Annual plans are billed upfront.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/billing-and-payments#schedule
- `billing_payments.change_plan`
  - **Question**: Can I change my plan later?
  - **Answer**: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes
    take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/billing-and-payments#change-plan

## Refunds & Cancellations

- `refunds_cancellations.refund_policy`
  - **Question**: What is AcmeFlow's refund policy?
  - **Answer**: AcmeFlow offers refunds within 30 days of the initial purchase
    for first-time customers only.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/refunds-and-cancellations#refund-policy
- `refunds_cancellations.cancel_subscription`
  - **Question**: How do I cancel my subscription?
  - **Answer**: You can cancel your subscription from your account settings.
    Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/refunds-and-cancellations#cancel
- `refunds_cancellations.charges_after_cancel`
  - **Question**: Will I be charged after I cancel?
  - **Answer**: No. Once canceled, you will not be charged for subsequent
    billing periods.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/refunds-and-cancellations#after-cancel

## Account Management

- `account_management.add_or_remove_users`
  - **Question**: How do I add or remove users?
  - **Answer**: Admins can add or remove users from the Admin Dashboard at any
    time.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/account-management#add-remove-users
- `account_management.remove_user_effect`
  - **Question**: What happens when I remove a user?
  - **Answer**: Removed users lose access immediately, but their historical
    activity remains available for audit purposes.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/account-management#remove-user
- `account_management.transfer_ownership`
  - **Question**: Can I transfer account ownership?
  - **Answer**: Yes. Account ownership can be transferred by an existing admin.
  - **Source**:
    https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/account-management#transfer-ownership

## Data & Security

- `data_security.data_retention`
  - **Question**: How long does AcmeFlow retain customer data?
  - **Answer**: AcmeFlow retains customer data for the duration of the active
    subscription.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/data-and-security#retention
- `data_security.after_cancel`
  - **Question**: What happens to my data after I cancel?
  - **Answer**: Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it
    is permanently deleted.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/data-and-security#after-cancel
- `data_security.encryption`
  - **Question**: Is my data encrypted?
  - **Answer**: Yes. AcmeFlow encrypts data in transit and at rest using
    industry-standard encryption.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/data-and-security#encryption
- `data_security.soc2`
  - **Question**: Is AcmeFlow SOC 2 compliant?
  - **Answer**: Yes. AcmeFlow is SOC 2 Type II compliant.
  - **Source**: https://support.acmeflow.com/faq/data-and-security#soc2
