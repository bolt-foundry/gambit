# Introducing Gambit Posts

**Published:** 2026-01-19

## What changed

- Created `docs/posts/` as the timeline for notable Gambit updates.
- Added guidance on when to publish (launches, milestone wraps, research drops).
- Wired posts to reference internal execution notes so readers can jump from
  announcements to the living plans.

## Why

We needed a lightweight way to broadcast outcomes without forcing teammates to
trawl every memo. Posts give us a chronological digest while keeping memos
focused on in-progress work.
